What is DraftSeason?

DraftSeason is a full stack Web App where users can run MockDraft simulations. Users can customize their draft experience by changing settings including the following:

  • Teams Selected
  • Number of Rounds
  • Speed of Simulation
The simulation is built upon a complex scoring algorithm that is taylor made for each team and their real life draft philosophies and positional needs.
